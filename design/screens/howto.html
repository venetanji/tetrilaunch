Briefing

How to Play

01
Aim & charge

Drag from the cannon — direction sets the angle, distance sets the power. Release to fire. On desktop use WS to aim, AD for power.

02
Rotate the piece

Spin the incoming tetromino with the ⟲/⟳ buttons or QE before launch.

03
Watch the arc

The dotted parabola previews exactly where the piece flies. Pieces are joined by breakable joints — hard hits shatter them.

04
Fill the rows

Land enough cubes in a row on the right of the compactor to complete a line.

05
Beat the compactor

The red bar sweeps right, clearing full rows for points. Cubes stuck on its left blink out and cost you.

06
Hit 800

Reach the target score to clear Launch Bay. More levels & roguelite mutators are coming.
